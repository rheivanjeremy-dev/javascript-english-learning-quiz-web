01 — Pengantar Materi

Apa itu Direct &
Indirect Speech?

Dalam bahasa Inggris, ada dua cara untuk melaporkan apa yang dikatakan seseorang. Kenali perbedaannya di sini.

💬
Direct Speech

Kalimat langsung — menuliskan atau mengucapkan kembali kata-kata seseorang persis seperti aslinya, diapit tanda kutip (" ").

🗣️
Indirect Speech

Kalimat tidak langsung — melaporkan apa yang dikatakan seseorang tanpa kutipan, dengan menyesuaikan tenses, kata ganti, dan keterangan waktu.

🎯
Perbedaan Utama

Direct speech menggunakan tanda kutip dan kata asli pembicara. Indirect speech menghapus tanda kutip, mengubah tenses (backshift), dan menyesuaikan pronoun serta time expression.

02 — Penjelasan & Contoh

Memahami
Setiap Jenis

Pelajari struktur dan contoh kalimat untuk setiap jenis speech berikut ini.

DIRECT Direct Speech

Definisi: Kalimat yang mengutip langsung ucapan seseorang. Ditandai dengan tanda kutip (" ") dan biasanya diawali dengan kata kerja seperti say, said, tell, told, ask, asked.

Struktur: Subject + said/told + "…kata asli…"

CONTOH 1
She said, "I am happy."
CONTOH 2
He said, "I will go to school tomorrow."
CONTOH 3
They said, "We are studying English now."

INDIRECT Indirect Speech

Definisi: Kalimat yang melaporkan ucapan seseorang tanpa menggunakan kutipan langsung. Tenses berubah (backshift), pronoun disesuaikan, dan time expression ikut berubah.

Struktur: Subject + said/told + that + clause (tenses mundur satu)

CONTOH 1 (dari She said, "I am happy.")
She said that she was happy.
CONTOH 2 (dari He said, "I will go to school tomorrow.")
He said that he would go to school the next day.
CONTOH 3 (dari They said, "We are studying English now.")
They said that they were studying English then.

DIRECT Kalimat Tanya (Questions)

Untuk kalimat tanya, gunakan kata kerja asked dan kata tanya (wh-word) atau if/whether untuk yes/no questions.

YES/NO QUESTION
He asked, "Are you tired?" → He asked if I was tired.
WH- QUESTION
She asked, "Where do you live?" → She asked where I lived.

INDIRECT Kalimat Perintah (Commands)

Untuk kalimat perintah, gunakan told + to + verb atau asked + to + verb untuk permintaan sopan.

COMMAND
"Close the door!" → He told me to close the door.
REQUEST
"Please open the window." → She asked me to open the window.
